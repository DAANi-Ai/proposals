Section 1

Understanding Your Situation

Ruth has built something remarkable: a thriving coaching practice spanning six countries, built entirely on trust and word of mouth. That is not an easy thing to achieve. But the very success that makes this business special is now creating a serious operational problem.

3,000+
Active clients across Mexico, US, Panama, Israel, Brazil, and the UK
10-20
Hours every week spent manually answering WhatsApp messages
6+
Countries, time zones, languages, and pricing structures to manage
The real cost of manual messaging
At 15 hours a week, Ruth spends roughly two full working days every week just answering messages. That is time she cannot spend on coaching, creating content, or taking on new clients. The human assistant helps, but requires constant guidance -- which means the bottleneck is Ruth herself, not the volume of messages.

The current setup has no unified system. Scheduling lives in Confirmafy (which syncs with Google Calendar -- that is a good foundation). Payments are tracked manually. Client details are scattered across chats. There is no quick way for Ruth to see which clients are current, which are waiting, or which messages still need replies.

The opportunity here is significant. An AI assistant trained on how Ruth writes and thinks -- one that can classify clients, answer common questions, handle scheduling, track payments, and give Ruth a clear daily summary -- would give her back 10 or more hours a week. That is time she can put toward the work that only she can do.

Section 2

What We Will Build - Phase 1

Phase 1 is a complete WhatsApp AI assistant that handles the operational side of Ruth's business. Every feature below is purpose-built for a solo coaching practice with a high-volume, multi-country client base.

  • 1
    Bilingual AI Assistant - English and Spanish
    The assistant detects the language each client writes in and responds in kind. No client ever needs to switch language or feel like they are talking to a foreign system. Fully fluent in both languages, with natural phrasing throughout.
  • 2
    Tone-Matched Responses
    The assistant is trained on Ruth's actual messages, so it writes the way Ruth writes - warm, direct, professional. Clients will not notice the difference. Before going live, Ruth reviews and approves response samples until they feel right.
  • 3
    Smart Message Filtering
    Personal contacts, family, and friends are excluded completely. The assistant only handles business-related conversations. Ruth defines the rules, and anything unclear is flagged for her attention rather than responded to automatically.
  • 4
    Client Classification System
    Every client is tagged automatically: country, pricing tier, session type, payment status, and current programme stage. Ruth can see at a glance exactly who is who, without digging through chat history.
  • 5
    Scheduling Integration via Google Calendar
    The assistant bridges with Confirmafy through Google Calendar. When a client asks to book, reschedule, or confirm a session, the assistant checks availability and handles it. Ruth's calendar stays accurate without her involvement.
  • 6
    Payment Tracking and Automated Reminders
    Outstanding payments are tracked per client. The assistant sends polite, personalised reminders at the right intervals, in the right language, in Ruth's voice. No more chasing manually.
  • 7
    Waitlist Management
    New enquiries that come in when Ruth is at capacity go onto a managed waitlist. The system keeps interested clients warm, sends updates when spots open, and converts enquiries into booked sessions automatically.
  • 8
    Daily Summary Reports for Ruth
    Each morning, Ruth receives a concise summary of what happened the day before: messages handled, bookings made, payments received, anything flagged for her review. One clear view, no digging required.
  • 9
    Admin Dashboard
    A simple, private web dashboard where Ruth (or a trusted assistant) can see everything in one place: active clients, pending payments, upcoming sessions, flagged messages, and system status. Available on any device.

Section 4

How It Works - Project Timeline

From first call to live assistant in five weeks. The timeline is structured so Ruth is never left waiting without knowing what is happening next.

  • 1
    Week 1
    Discovery Call and Data Collection
    A 30-60 minute call with Daniel to map Ruth's full business: message types, client categories, pricing tiers, scheduling workflow, payment process, and anything else the assistant needs to know. Ruth provides sample messages (anonymised), her Confirmafy access, and any existing client lists or notes. This is where the final quote is confirmed.
  • 2
    Weeks 2 and 3
    Build and Training
    The assistant is built and trained on Ruth's actual communication style. Integrations are connected (WhatsApp Business API, Google Calendar, Confirmafy bridge). Client classification rules are configured. The admin dashboard is set up with Ruth's client data imported.
  • 3
    Week 4
    Testing with Real Messages
    Ruth reviews a set of real message scenarios and checks the assistant's responses. Any tone, phrasing, or classification adjustments are made here. This stage does not end until Ruth is satisfied that the responses sound like her. Nothing goes live without her approval.
  • 4
    Week 5
    Go Live and Handover
    The assistant goes live. Ruth receives a short walkthrough of the admin dashboard, the daily summary format, and how to flag messages for personal attention. A written handover guide is provided. For Options B and C, two weeks of post-launch support begins here.
  • ∞
    Ongoing
    Support as Per Chosen Option
    Option A: updates and fixes charged as needed. Option B: two weeks included, then standard rate. Option C: continuous optimisation, priority support, and monthly performance reviews included in the monthly fee.
Section 5

Phase 2 Vision

Phase 1 solves the operational problem. Phase 2 transforms the business model. Once the AI assistant is live and Ruth has reclaimed her time, the next question is: what could this business look like if the infrastructure were built for scale?

Phase 2 will be scoped and priced after Phase 1 is live and delivering results. Nothing below is included in Phase 1 pricing.

AI-Powered Pre-Sessions
Before each coaching session, the AI prepares a brief for Ruth: client history, last session notes, goals, and any WhatsApp exchanges since the previous session. Ruth walks into every call fully prepared, automatically.
Client Self-Service Portal
A private web portal where clients can view their session history, book their next appointment, access shared resources, and manage their own payment method. Reduces inbound messages further and increases client satisfaction.
Full Coaching Platform
A branded app or web platform under Ruth's name. Group programmes, digital resources, intake forms, progress tracking, and payment processing all in one place. The foundation for productising her expertise beyond 1-on-1 coaching.
Video and Content Integration
If Ruth creates video content or audio resources, the platform can host and distribute them to clients as part of their programme. This opens the door to passive income products alongside her live coaching practice.
